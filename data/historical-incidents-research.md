## Landmark Supreme Court Cases

These cases shape the legal framework for evaluating gubernatorial conduct:

1. **S.R. Bommai v. Union of India (1994)** - Landmark; Art. 356 now judicially reviewable
2. **Rameshwar Prasad v. Union of India (2006)** - Bihar case; floor test principles
3. **Nabam Rebia v. Deputy Speaker (2016)** - Arunachal; governor cannot intervene in legislature
4. **State of Rajasthan v. Union of India (1977)** - Early federalism case
5. **State of Tamil Nadu v. Governor (2025)** - Deemed assent doctrine; timelines for governors
6. **State of Punjab v. Principal Secretary (2023)** - Governors cannot "pocket veto"
7. **State of Kerala v. Governor (2023-2025)** - Pending bills; governor must act
